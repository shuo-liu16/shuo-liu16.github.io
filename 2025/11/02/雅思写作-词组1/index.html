按主题分类的核心词组

技能

技能核心词组概览

  • develop/improve/boost their skills

    • independent thinking skills
    • problems-solving skills
    • social skills
    • teamwork skills
    • reading and writing skills
    • communication skills
    • computer skills
    • foreign language skills

  • develop good learning habits

    • learn more efficiently

  • deepen their understanding of …

    • gain acknowledge about …

  • boost their brain development

    • improve their problem-solving skills

  • encourage their imagination

    • encourage think creatively

  • expand/broaden their horizons

  • work closely together

  • increase their independence

    • help them prepare for employment
    • have more job opportunities

  • gain practical experience

  • achieve their potential

    • achieve their goals

一、教育万能核心词组（用于各类教育相关话题）

  • develop/improve [various] skills（可替换任何技能）
  • deepen their understanding of [a subject]
  • expand/broaden their horizons
  • achieve their potential/goals

二、个人能力与发展词组（用于能力培养、创新类话题）

  • independent thinking skills（用于强调批判性思维、创新）
  • problems-solving skills（用于应对挑战、实践类活动）
  • increase their independence（用于讨论责任感、自我管理）
  • encourage their imagination
  • encourage think creatively（用于艺术、科技、创新类话题）

三、社会与职业相关词组（用于社交、职业规划类话题）

  • social/teamwork skills（用于团队合作、沟通类话题）
  • work closely together（具体描述合作方式）
  • help them prepare for employment / have more job opportunities（讨论教育的目的、职业培训）
  • gain practical experience（对比理论知识与实践经验）

效率

效率核心词组概览

  • save time and energy

    • make learning more efficient
    • make shopping more efficient
    • make communicate efficient

  • make the best use of time

    • is a waste of time

  • boost efficiency

    • boost productivity

  • boost their performance

  • make life more convenient and comfortable

    • technological innovations
    • artificial intelligence
    • self-driving cars

  • find information quickly and easily

    • provide a variety of information

  • have a competitive edge

    • face tough competition for jobs

一、时间管理核心词组（用于时间分配、任务规划类话题）

  • save time and energy
  • make the best use of time
  • is a waste of time

二、效率提升相关词组（用于工作、学习、生产类场景）

  • boost efficiency
  • boost productivity
  • boost their performance
  • make learning more efficient
  • make shopping more efficient
  • make communicate efficient

三、便捷与竞争相关词组（用于科技、职场、生活类话题）

  • make life more convenient and comfortable（关联 technological innovations / artificial intelligence / self-driving cars）
  • find information quickly and easily
  • provide a variety of information
  • have a competitive edge
  • face tough competition for jobs

权力

权力核心词组概览

  • have more freedom
    • respect their choices
  • should be a personal choice
    • a free and fair society
  • provide people with more food/shopping choices
  • can achieve a work-life balance
    • often work overtime
    • cannot achieve a work-life balance
  • have work security
    • have a steady source of income
  • are treated fairly/unfairly
  • have equal opportunities
    • feel respected and valued
  • boost gender quality
    • traditional gender roles
  • give them a sense of achievement
    • have a sense of belonging

一、个人自主相关词组（用于个人选择、自由权利类话题）

  • have more freedom
  • respect their choices
  • should be a personal choice
  • provide people with more food/shopping choices

二、职场权益相关词组（用于工作保障、生活平衡类场景）

  • can achieve a work-life balance
  • often work overtime
  • cannot achieve a work-life balance
  • have work security
  • have a steady source of income

三、公平与平等相关词组（用于社会公正、性别平等类话题）

  • are treated fairly/unfairly
  • have equal opportunities
  • feel respected and valued
  • boost gender equality
  • traditional gender roles

四、心理价值相关词组（用于个人认同、社会归属类场景）

  • give them a sense of achievement
  • have a sense of belonging
  • a free and fair society
