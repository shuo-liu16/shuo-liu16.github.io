按主题分类的核心词组

技能

一、教育万能核心词组

  • develop/improve [various] skills（可替换任何技能）
  • deepen their understanding of [a subject]
  • expand/broaden their horizons
  • achieve their potential/goals

二、个人能力与发展词组

  • independent thinking skills（用于强调批判性思维、创新）
  • problem-solving skills（用于应对挑战、实践类活动）
  • increase their independence（用于讨论责任感、自我管理）
  • encourage imagination/think creatively（用于艺术、科技、创新类话题）

三、社会与职业相关词组

  • social/teamwork skills（用于团队合作、沟通类话题）
  • work closely together（具体描述合作方式）
  • prepare for employment / job opportunities（讨论教育的目的、职业培训）
  • gain practical experience（对比理论知识与实践经验）

四、核心词组概览

  • develop/improve/boost their skills

    • independent thinking skills
    • problems-solving skills
    • social skills
    • teamwork skills
    • reading and writing skills
    • computer skills
    • foreign language skills

  • develop good learning habits

    • learn more efficiently

  • deepen their understanding of …

    • gain acknowledgement about …

  • boost their brain development

    • improve their problem-solving skills

  • encourage their imagination

    • encourage think creatively

  • expand/broaden their horizons

  • work closely together

  • increase their independence

    • help them prepare for employment
    • have more job opportunities

  • gain practical experience

  • achieve their potential

    • achieve their goals
